Bitter Leaf Soup

ingredients

  • Washed Bitter Leaf
  • Palm oil
  • salt
  • maggi
  • cocoayam
  • fresh meat and smoked fish

Steps to prepare

  1. boil the meat and fish for 15 minutes
  2. add maggi and salt
  3. add palm oil
  4. oil to cook for 5 minutes
  5. add bitter leaf and allow to simmer for 10 minutes
  6. serve and enjoy with semovita
